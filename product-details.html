This is Teyvat, a fantasy world where the seven elements meet. In the distant past, through faith in the gods, people were given the power to drive the elements and build homes in the wilderness. Five hundred years ago, the fall of the ancient kingdom caused the heavens and the earth to mutate...... Now, the catastrophe that has swept the continent has stopped, but peace has not yet arrived. As the protagonist of the story, you drift from outside the world and descend to the earth.
